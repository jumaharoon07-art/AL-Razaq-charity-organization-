Our Aim

The AL-RAZAQ Charity Organization is dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities by delivering essential services such as food aid, clean water access, healthcare support, education, and emergency relief. We operate locally to ensure every donation reaches those who need it most, bringing hope, dignity, and sustainable support to individuals and families in need.
